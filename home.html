Productivity Analysis is our flagship feature that transforms your browsing data into actionable insights. It includes: - Focus Score: Measure deep work vs. distracted time - Daily Digests: AI-generated summaries of your research activity - Trend Detection: Identify patterns in your productivity - Node Visualization: See connections between your research topics All analysis happens locally with pure JavaScript, so your data never leaves your device unless you explicitly save it to IPFS.

Our AI summaries are generated using a Python/Flask backend with custom NLP models. The summarization process extracts key concepts, generates concise overviews, and links related content through our knowledge graph. All summaries are stored on IPFS with their content hashes recorded on-chain for verification and permanence.
